In the tradition of Halton Arp's landmark 1966 Atlas of Peculiar Galaxies, this page presents a collection of galaxies with unusual morphologies identified using AION-Search, a semantic search engine for galaxy images trained on AI-generated captions. The comments accompanying each image were written by the author and are not AI-generated.
